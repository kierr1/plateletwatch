About PlateletWatch

A thesis project combining a microscope adapter with AI-powered platelet counting for early dengue diagnosis. The microscope captures a blood smear; our system counts individual platelet cells, estimates the total /µL, and maps the result against WHO severity grades — all in real time.

Mobile Microscope

Clip-on adapter for Android/iOS smartphones. 40× magnification captures platelets (small oval fragments, 2–4 µm) visible between red blood cells.

AI Platelet Counter

CNN model trained on annotated blood smears. Detects each platelet cell individually, numbers them on-screen, and estimates total /µL count with 94.7% accuracy.

WHO Severity Scale

Automatically maps platelet count to dengue severity (Normal / Low / Danger / Critical) per 2009 WHO guidelines, with clinical action guidance.

Exports / Reports

Clinical PDF and annotated images.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live platelet count tracking with automated threshold alerts. Push notifications when counts drop below clinical danger levels during dengue illness days.

RA 10173 Compliant

All patient data encrypted at rest and in transit. Role-based access for clinicians, lab techs, and patients. Philippine Data Privacy Act compliant.
