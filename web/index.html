What it does

Built for confident, early decisions

Every part of the workflow — from capture to report — is designed to keep clinicians fast, informed, and in control.

  • Real-time platelet tracking

    Live counts from microscope frames, charted over each session so trends surface immediately.

  • Automated risk stratification

    Counts are mapped to clear four-tier dengue risk bands, with thresholds you can review at a glance.

  • AI-assisted blood analysis

    Image models help identify and count platelets in smears, reducing manual effort and variability.

  • Secure patient records

    Patient data is encrypted and access-controlled — and is never sold or shared.
